16 Most Famous places to go

What makes a classic American city? Is it the food, the culture, the history? The answer is different for each and every iconic destination. From coast to coast, this country is filled with famous US cities that shouldn’t be missed. Let’s take a look at the 16 most famous places in the world.

1.Eiffel tower

TThe Eiffel Tower, or Tour Eiffel, is a wrought iron tower in Paris, France that was built in 1889 for the World's Fair, the Exposition Universelle. It's the most visited paid attraction in the world, with over 7 million visitors annually, and is considered a symbol of Paris.

2.Great wall of china

The Great Wall of China is a series of walls and fortifications that stretches across northern China and southern Mongolia. It is considered one of the world's construction wonders, and is a national symbol of China and a major tourist attraction.
